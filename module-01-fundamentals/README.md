# IBM MQ Bootcamp

## Module 01 - Fundamentals

Foundations of Enterprise Messaging and IBM MQ

---

## Overview

This module introduces the fundamental concepts of enterprise messaging and IBM MQ.

Students learn why messaging platforms exist, how IBM MQ enables reliable communication between systems and why messaging remains a critical component of modern enterprise architectures.

The module establishes the conceptual foundation required for administration, security, troubleshooting, high availability and modernization topics covered throughout the remainder of the bootcamp.

## Module Contents

### Chapter 01 - Enterprise Messaging Fundamentals

Why messaging matters, business drivers and integration challenges.

### Chapter 02 - Evolution of Enterprise Integration

How enterprise integration evolved from batch processing to event-driven architectures.

### Chapter 03 - Why IBM MQ Exists

The business and technical challenges IBM MQ was designed to solve.

### Chapter 04 - IBM MQ Architecture Overview

Core IBM MQ components and architecture fundamentals.

### Chapter 05 - Messaging Patterns

Point-to-Point, Request/Reply and Publish/Subscribe.

### Chapter 06 - Message Delivery Guarantees

At Most Once, At Least Once and Exactly Once delivery models.

### Chapter 07 - IBM MQ vs REST vs Kafka

Architectural comparison and positioning guidance.

### Chapter 08 - IBM MQ Objects

Queues, channels and object fundamentals.

### Chapter 09 - Introduction to MQMD

Message metadata and message tracking concepts.

### Chapter 10 - Message Flow

Understanding how messages move through IBM MQ.

### Chapter 11 - Persistence and Transactions

Transactional integrity, persistence and recovery fundamentals.

### Chapter 12 - IBM MQ in Modern Architectures

IBM MQ within APIs, events, cloud and hybrid integration environments.

### Chapter 13 - IBM MQ in the Enterprise

Real-world enterprise use cases and business adoption.

### Chapter 14 - Module Summary

Review of key concepts and preparation for hands-on activities.
