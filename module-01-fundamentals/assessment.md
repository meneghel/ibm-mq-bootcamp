# Question 01

Why do messaging platforms exist?

A) To replace databases

B) To provide reliable communication between systems

C) To replace APIs

D) To replace applications

## Answer

B

## Explanation

Messaging platforms provide reliable, asynchronous and loosely coupled communication between applications.
